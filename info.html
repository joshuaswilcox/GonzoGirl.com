There is something exciting about freezing 1/500th of a second
forever in time. That one great shot is the same challenge I’ve
been chasing since the first time I looked through the viewfinder.

The very trick in hooking a moment seems mostly luck while
juggling focus, frame, and exposure. It’s never really that I see
a good subject - but rather a good subject sees me.
